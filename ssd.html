Crucial P3 Plus 500GB PCIe M.2 NVMe Gen 4 SSD CT500P3PSSD8

Overview : 

Maximize the performance of your system with the 500GB Crucial P3 Plus NVMe PCIe 4.0 M.2 Internal SSD. Designed for professionals, enthusiasts, and gamers, this internal solid-state drive is designed to deliver up to 43% greater performance than gen 3 drives with sequential read and write speeds of 4700 MB/s and 1900 MB/s. Installing this SSD into an available M.2 2280 PCIe 4.0 x4 slot allows you to load demanding applications, large project files, and complex games in a flash. It is also backward compatible with M.2 PCIe 3.0 slots with reduced speed.

The Crucial P3 Plus SSD uses Micron Advanced 176-layer 3D NAND technology and includes advanced features such as dynamic write acceleration, error correction code (ECC), TRIM support, and self-monitoring and reporting technology (SMART) to help ensure data integrity and high performance. With a 1.5-million hour MTBF rating, you can enjoy extended longevity.
