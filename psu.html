120MM SILENT HYDRAULIC BEARING (HYB) FAN

The 120mm fan detects the current power utilization and temperature, automatically adjusting its rotational speed to minimize noise.The Hydraulic Bearing fan offers a longer and more stable lifespan in comparison to traditional sleeve fan.

PROTECTION

To guarantee the stable operation of your entire computer system under any circumstances, we have incorporated a range of protective features including OCP, OTP, OVP, OPP, UVP, and SCP. Moreover, we have obtained safety certifications from various countries to ensure the consistent and secure functioning of your system.

80 PLUS GOLD CERTIFIED

The 80 Plus Gold certification guarantees a 90% efficiency rate at a 50% load, resulting in reduced power wastage, diminished heat generation, and minimized fan noise.

COMPACT DESIGN

Through GIGABYTE’s exceptional design capabilities and the use of high-quality materials, we have successfully reduced the size of the power supply unit. This compact power supply not only facilitates easier installation into various chassis but also enhances the thermal efficiency within the case
